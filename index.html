Welcome to Web 101

Welcome to Web 101, your go-to resource for learning the fundamentals of web development. This website is designed for beginners who are eager to learn how to create beautiful and functional websites from scratch. Whether you are completely new to web development or looking to brush up on your skills, Web 101 has you covered.

Our curriculum is divided into three main sections:

  • HTML: Learn how to structure your web pages using the building blocks of the web.
  • CSS: Discover how to style your web pages to make them visually appealing and responsive.
  • JavaScript: Dive into programming to add interactivity and dynamic content to your websites.

Each section is filled with easy-to-follow tutorials, examples, and exercises to help you practice and master your web development skills. Let's get started on your journey to becoming a web developer!
